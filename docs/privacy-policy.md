# Ostomate Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-06-09
**App:** Ostomate (iOS + Android)
**Developer:** Bobby Helco — bhelco@gmail.com

## Data Collection

Ostomate does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
All information you enter — supply change events, inventory counts, and settings — is stored
exclusively on your device using the local database (SQLite). No data ever leaves your device.

## Crash Reporting

Release builds of Ostomate include Firebase Crashlytics, which collects anonymous,
non-identifiable crash reports. These reports contain only technical information
(stack traces, device model, OS version) and never include your supply tracking data,
inventory counts, or any personally identifiable information.

You may opt out of crash reporting by uninstalling the app.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties.
There is no account system. There is no server. Your data is yours.
