Ostomate — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your data stays on your device

Ostomate stores all your supply and change event data locally on this device using a private database. Your health data is never sent to any server, cloud service, or third party. The only thing that can leave your device is an optional crash report — see below.

No analytics or tracking

Ostomate contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no telemetry of any kind. We do not know how you use the app.

No account required

Ostomate works without creating an account or providing any personal information. Your name, email, and identity are never collected.

Backups

The Export feature creates a backup file on your device that you can save wherever you choose. Ostomate does not upload backups anywhere automatically.

Crash reporting (optional)

Ostomate includes optional crash reporting powered by Sentry, which is disabled by default. If you choose to enable it in Settings → Security, only crash stack traces are sent — no personal data, no usage events, and your supply or event notes are never included. You can disable it at any time.

Data deletion

Uninstalling Ostomate removes all app data from your device. There is no data stored elsewhere to delete.
