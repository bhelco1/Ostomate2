Ostomate is a private, local-first ostomy supply tracker built for everyday use.

STAYING STOCKED SHOULDN'T REQUIRE A SPREADSHEET

Ostomate learns your change frequency and predicts when each supply is running low — so you get a reminder before you're caught short, not after.

WHAT IT DOES

• One-tap logging — record a change in under two seconds from the home screen, a home screen widget, or by scanning a QR sticker you print once and stick anywhere
• Smart predictions — average change interval updates automatically with every log; days-remaining estimates improve over time as Ostomate learns your rhythm
• Inventory tracking — on-hand counts update when you log; the home screen shows a warning banner when you're running low
• Calendar view — see your full change history at a glance; tap any day for a detail sheet where you can edit or delete events
• Stats — weekly, monthly, and yearly summaries; per-supply counts and average interval; trend sparkline
• Custom supplies — add any ostomy product beyond the built-in bag and flange types; set your own box sizes, reorder thresholds, and color coding
• Event notes and tags — optionally attach a note (leak, skin irritation, routine) to any logged change for richer history
• Backup and restore — export your full history as a CSV and restore it on a new device; imports from Ostomate v1 too

PRIVACY FIRST

Everything stays on your device. No account required. No iCloud sync. No analytics or tracking of any kind. Your health data is yours alone.

ACCESSIBLE

Full VoiceOver support — every button, card, and control is labeled and reachable by screen reader. High-contrast color pairs, minimum 44 pt touch targets throughout.

FREE — no subscriptions, no ads, no in-app purchases.
